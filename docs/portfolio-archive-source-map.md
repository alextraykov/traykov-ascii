## Evidence Policy

Safe measured claims from Pendo:

- Pipelines AI step creation: `1,124` accounts, `1,973` visitors, `4,087` events for Generate Steps AI; `1,000` accounts, `1,835` visitors, `4,727` events for Populate all steps.
- Bulk Trigger: `61` accounts, `74` visitors, `129` events.
- ALM Environments: `134` accounts created environments; `77` accounts published/deployed changes.
- Pipelines app-level PES: PES `33.4`, adoption `33.3`, stickiness `48.1`, growth `18.7`. Use as context only.
- Platform NPS `28.8` is whole-platform context only; do not attribute it to a feature or to Alex.

Targets, not measured outcomes:

- ALM deploy success `>=95%`, failure `<=2%`, CES `>=5.5/7`, CSAT `>=4/5`.
- Pave time-to-first-value, app creation completion, activation, retention, or revenue impact.
- Mini-Hack programme goals such as 60% teams producing working prototypes and 2 prototypes adopted/incubated per quarter.
- SynapseSys adoption, retention, activation, generated-node reuse, or usability findings.

Avoid:

- Turning event counts into success rates, revenue, productivity, satisfaction, retention, or ROI.
- Claiming measured Pave customer impact without separate telemetry.
- Claiming Figma MCP/code pipeline as a shipped platform capability; label it POC.
- Claiming designer-gated Claude Design as shipped adoption; label it draft/open feedback.
- Publishing raw Jira, Confluence, Slack, M365, customer emails, personal OAuth screenshots, private repo links, roadmap dates, or confidential hiring/review content.
